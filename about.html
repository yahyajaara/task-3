Trio Shawarma Restaurant is located in the city of Nablus, near the old An-Najah University building. It is a relatively modern restaurant. Trio specializes in the world of shawarma and meat, where you will find very rich dishes and meals served in a delicious way. Here you will find Bobcat shawarma, Bulldozer, and Bulldozer Cheese, and you have the option of ordering the type of meat from chicken or Even red meat to suit your taste.
